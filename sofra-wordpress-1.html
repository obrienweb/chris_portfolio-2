Overview

The Client

SOFRA is an app design that I created as a personal project to bring the skills that I have learned into a project that allows me to expand upon them and get creative. It is an e-commerce furniture store app that dynamically displays the products and a simple flow from start to end.

The Challenge

Designing SOFRA has some unexpected challenges that really got me thinking about how a user would use the app instead of just how I see the app. By using the personas, I was able to go back and think about how they would use the app and what the user would expect from it. Through this process, I was able to design a mobile-centric shop that showcases products and streamlines the purchasing process.

The Process

As a starting point, I presented different personas for the average user that would be interested in purchasing furniture through an online or app platform. Once the target demo was found, I conducted market research on competitorsâ€™ apps and websites to further investigate the current web and app offerings. From there, I was able to what works and what could be improved upon from these other apps and develop wireframes of the different components in the app. I then created the prototype of SOFRA with all the features available and with testing, I was able to see what I got right and what could still be improved upon.

The Outcome

SOFRA is now an easy to use and aesthetically appealing app that allows users to quickly find furniture for their needs. Having multiple types of furniture available helps users to see what all of their options are and then be able to choose and learn more about that product.

Final Product

Home

From the landing page the user can Log In or Sign Up to continue shopping. The Log In and Sign Up pages are simple and easy use so the user will convert faster to having an account and therefore streamlining the process.

Shopping

SOFRA is a simple and straight forward app that allows users to find the perfect furniture for their home. From the homepage the user can shop by category, suggested, or best sellers. After selecting the perfect furniture from the list or items, they can see more images and find a thorough description of the product. Using the Google Sheets plugin, I was able to easily and dynamically update the content for the prototype.

Checkout

Checking out has never been easier with the streamlined checkout process in SOFRA. From the shopping cart, to adding a different address or payment information, it is all easily found and updated if needed. After they checkout, the user can find suggested products based on their previous purchase.

Account

If a user wants to update their account settings, they can do it all from the My Account page. They can update their profile information right on the first page. My Orders is where they can find their past purchases. If they have selected any favorite products, they can find them all on the Favorites page.
